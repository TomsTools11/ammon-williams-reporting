05

Launch plan

1
Confirm final targeting scope — today's call
ZIP list + current-carrier mix as provided June 10, or statewide with carrier filters. Scope determines final bid calibration (ZIP-restricted scope adds 20–30% to base bids).
2
Confirm operations — today's call
Budget split (proposed ~55/45 Home/Auto), lead notification email(s), a direct call line that bypasses the phone tree, daily schedule, and current close rates for the CPA baseline.
3
Launch
Leads map into Allstate Lead Manager with TrustedForm consent and session replay on every lead. The $200 launch fund applies to the first clicks. Days 1–3: verify click flow. Days 4–7: review win rate by source.
4
Recalibrate on data
Week 2: fresh market-pricing pull and bid review. Day 30: full re-price on Lead Manager disposition export (contact, quote, bound). Bi-weekly account reviews thereafter; optimization targets CPA, contact rate, and quote rate.
